Human Foods That Dogs Can or Cannot Eat: A Comprehensive Guide

As much as we love to share our food with our furry friends, not all human foods are safe for dogs to consume. In fact, some foods can be toxic and cause serious health problems in dogs.
Therefore, it's important to be aware of what human foods are safe and which ones to avoid feeding to your dog. Foods that are safe for dogs to eat include lean meats such as chicken and
turkey, certain fruits like apples and bananas, and vegetables like carrots and green beans. However, it's important to make sure that these foods are cooked and not seasoned with any
harmful ingredients such as garlic or onion powder.On the other hand, there are many human foods that dogs should avoid at all costs. These include chocolate, caffeine, alcohol, grapes,
raisins, and anything sweetened with xylitol, which can be deadly for dogs even in small amounts. In general, it's best to stick to a balanced and nutritionally complete dog food diet
recommended by your veterinarian. If you want to give your dog some human food as a treat or supplement, make sure to do so in moderation and always double-check that the food is safe
for your furry friend to eat.
